Corpers Lodge

Corper's lodge is a modern-day story about new corpers who have just been deployed to their places of primary assignment. Femi, a youth pastor who has gone rogue; and Emeka, his trusted sidekick and personal clown. we also have the lodge's pastor, Clara. And I definitely didn't forget, Daniel, a young man who fought to fulfil his calling even if that meant letting go of his own plans.

The series was realistic and had me hooked from the very first episode. We all know that content creation is the trendy profession in Nigeria right now. While some people like Clara use it carefully and don't let the attention get to their heads, we have other people like Amanda whose heads have literally blown up with pride.
